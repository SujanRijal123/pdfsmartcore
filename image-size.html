How to Use Image Size Tool

Step-by-step guide for resizing images

  • Step 1: Upload an image file (JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP)
  • Step 2: Use the slider to adjust size from 1% to 500%
  • Step 3: Quick presets: 50% (reduce), 150% (increase), or Reset to 100%
  • Step 4: See real-time size calculation in the display box
  • Step 5: Click "Adjust Image Size" to process and download

Tip: Aspect ratio is automatically maintained. Image quality is preserved as much as possible.

Security: 100% secure and private. Images are automatically deleted after processing.
