How to Use PDF to JPG Converter

Step-by-step guide for extracting images from PDF

  • Step 1: Upload a PDF file
  • Step 2: Click on page thumbnails to select/deselect pages
  • Step 3: Use "Select All" or "Clear All" buttons for quick selection
  • Step 4: Click "Extract Selected Images" to convert pages to JPG
  • Step 5: Download ZIP file containing all extracted images

Tip: If no pages are selected, all pages will be converted by default.
