How to Use PDF Size Tool

Step-by-step guide for compressing PDF file size

  • Step 1: Upload a PDF file using the upload area or drag & drop
  • Step 2: Choose quality level: High (20-30% reduction), Medium (30-50% reduction), or Low (50-70% reduction)
  • Step 3: See estimated size and reduction percentage in real-time
  • Step 4: Click "Compress PDF" to process and download

Tip: High quality is best for important documents, Low quality for email attachments.

Security: 100% secure and private. Files never leave your device.

Note: Final file size may vary based on PDF content complexity.
